# Credit U™ Handout: Common Credit Report Errors

**Module**: Junior Module 01
**Goal**: Identify and highlight errors on your report.

## 🔎 What to Look For

Check your credit report for these 5 common errors:

1. **Wrong Name/Address**: Personal info that doesn't belong to you.
2. **Duplicate Accounts**: The same debt listed twice.
3. **Incorrect Balance**: Showing a balance on a paid-off account.
4. **Wrong Late Payments**: Payments you made on time marked as late.
5. **Zombie Debt**: Negative items older than 7 years still showing.

## 🛠️ Action Step

Using a red pen or digital highlighter:

- [ ] Circle any name variations that are not yours.
- [ ] Circle any address where you have never lived.
- [ ] Highlight any account balance that looks incorrect.

**Next Step**: Use the CUAI **MOV Letter** if these items are verified as "accurate" by the bureau.
