## 🛡️ [MOV] Method of Verification Demand

**Statute**: FCRA § 611(a)(7)
**Target**: Generic "Verified" responses from bureaus.

> I am formally requesting a description of the procedure used to determine the accuracy and completeness of the disputed information, including the business name and address of any furnisher contacted, and the name of the specific individual(s) with whom you verified this data. This information is required to be provided within 15 days of this request pursuant to FCRA § 611(a)(7).

## 🏚️ [BKY-01] Post-Discharge Audit Notice

**Statute**: 11 U.S.C. § 524
**Target**: Reporting a "Balance Owed" on a debt discharged in bankruptcy.

> NOTICE: The debt associated with this account was discharged in Bankruptcy Case [Case Number]. Federal law prohibits the reporting of a balance or "Past Due" amount on a discharged debt. You are hereby commanded to update the status to "Discharged in Bankruptcy" and set the balance to $0.00 immediately.

## 🛑 [BKY-02] Automatic Stay Violation (Cease & Desist)

**Statute**: 11 U.S.C. § 362
**Target**: Debt collectors calling during an active bankruptcy filing.

> WARNING: An automatic stay is currently in effect regarding Bankruptcy Case [Case Number]. Any further attempt to collect this debt is a direct violation of 11 U.S.C. § 362. Continued contact will result in an immediate motion for sanctions and attorney fees in Bankruptcy Court.

## 🏠 [BKY-03] Rule 3002.1 Compliance Demand

**Statute**: Fed. R. Bankr. V. 3002.1 (2026 Amended)
**Target**: Surprise mortgage fees in Chapter 13.

> DEMAND FOR DISCLOSURE: Pursuant to Rule 3002.1, the mortgage servicer is required to disclose all fees, expenses, and charges during the Chapter 13 plan. Failure to provide timely notice of these fees precludes the servicer from collecting them post-discharge. Provide a full accounting of all Rule 3002.1 notices filed to date.

## 🤝 [SS-01] Short Sale / DIL Status Correction

**Target**: Reporting "Foreclosure" on a voluntary asset transfer.

> FACTUAL DISPUTE: This account was resolved through a voluntary Short Sale (or Deed-in-Lieu of Foreclosure). Reporting this as a "Foreclosure" is inaccurate and damaging. Update the status to reflect "Settled" or "Voluntary Surrender/Short Sale" to comply with FCRA accuracy standards.
