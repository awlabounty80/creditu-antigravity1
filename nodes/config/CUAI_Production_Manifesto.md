# CUAI Video Production Manifesto

**Version**: 1.1 (Unique Asset Injection Protocol)
**Standards**: Elite Fintech / Luxury Branding

## 1. Aesthetics

- **Color Palette**: High-Contrast Dark Mode only.
  - Primary Navy: `#001F3F` (Credit U Legacy Navy)
  - Accent Gold: `#D4AF37` (Credit U Sovereign Gold)
  - Glow Indigo: `#4F46E5` (UI Elements)

- **Style Lock (Environment)**: All 3D assets/interfaces must be rendered on:
  - **Floor**: Polished Black Marble or Deep Navy reflective floor.
  - **Lighting**: Cinematic Gold Rim Lighting on objects.
  - **Background**: Infinite Dark Navy void or Minimalist Luxury Penthouse.

- **Style Lock (Characters)**:
  - **Apparel**: Deep Navy CUAI Hoodie, Tailored Blazers, or Quarter-zips mandatory.
  - **Branding**: High-visibility Golden 'CU' or 'CUAI' Crest on chest.
  - **Demographics**: Representative of a diverse HBCU campus (Black and Brown professionals, primarily Ages 25–65).

- **Visual Style**: Glassmorphism, subtle translucency, and continuous motion.

## 2. Character & Vibe Directive (The HBCU DNA)

The "HBCU vibe" is the atmosphere of ambition, community, and generational success.

### **The "Takeover" Prompt DNA**

> *"A cinematic, high-resolution photo capturing the energy of an 'HBCU Homecoming for Wealth.' A diverse group of Black and Brown professionals, aged 25–65, are smiling and networking on a historic red-brick campus quad. They are 'Credit University AI' students wearing premium **Credit Swag** (navy blue hoodies, quarter-zips, and tailored blazers with a gold metallic 'CUAI' crest). The central figure is a confident Black woman in a navy tailored blazer (Age 35), holding a gold tablet displaying a glowing '850' credit score dial. The atmosphere is optimistic, collaborative, and successful. Cinematic Golden Hour lighting casts a rich, metallic glow on the navy and gold surfaces."*

### **Visual Archetypes**

1. **The Community**: Group shots emphasizing collaborative success and legacy-building.
2. **The Mentor**: Distinguished elders (Age 50-65) representing wisdom and leadership.
3. **The Matrix Unplugged**: Younger professionals (Age 25-35) transitioning from "Algorithm Confusion" to "Sovereign Breakthrough."
4. **The Innovation Hub**: Historic red-brick architecture seamlessly integrated with modern glass and steel tech centers.

| **Phase 2: Sophomore** | Evidence & Auditing | Gold magnifying glasses, navy file folders, holographic data. |

## 3. Typography

- **Headers**: Inter or Barlow (Black/Bold) for mission-critical information.
  - Case: UPPERCASE for high-intensity sections.

- **Body**: JetBrains Mono or Inter (Regular) for monospaced technical data.

## 3. Transitions & Motion

- **Cross-Fade**: Mandatory 15-frame (0.5s) opacity fade between all visual assets.

- **Continuous Depth**: No static images allowed. Every visual must contain:
  - **Ken Burns**: Continuous slow zoom (1.0 to 1.15).
  - **Parallax Pan**: Subtle x-axis drift (0 to -20px over 20s).
  - **HUD Overlays**: Glowing, semi-transparent status indicators.

## 4. Content Sync

- **Visual Beats**: Every visual change must align with a "Script Beat" (defined in `timing_map.json`).

- **Timing**: 60-90 seconds max for tactical briefings. 2-5 minutes for core lectures.
